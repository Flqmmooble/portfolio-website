About me

I'm a 20-yo Cyber Security Student from Perth, Australia, working in SOC analytics.

I’m deeply engaged in both the offensive and defensive sides of cybersecurity, with a strong interest in red teaming techniques that target enterprise networks, web applications, and the often-overlooked niche attack vectors. I actively sharpen my skills through blue teaming labs (like BTLO) and hands-on SOC setups at home, building a well-rounded perspective on both attack and defense.

I’m also deeply interested in malware analysis and reverse engineering, always eager to understand threats at their core. In my spare time, you'll find me grinding through CTFs — RootMe is my current favorite — and I proudly represented my state at the Australian National WorldSkills Championships, earning the silver medal in Cyber Security.
